Appointments, privacy & visiting guidance

Clear expectations help us serve you safely and on time.

Appointments are recommended before you visit so we can match you with the right consultant and reduce waiting. Walk-ins are welcome when doctor availability allows.

Please arrive about 15 minutes early for registration. If you are more than 15 minutes late, your appointment may be delayed or rescheduled to protect other patients’ schedules.

To cancel or reschedule, call or message us on phone or WhatsApp, ideally at least 4 hours before your scheduled time.

Your personal details and medical records are treated as confidential. One attendant is generally permitted when clinically or practically needed.

On public holidays, OPD and some specialist hours may be reduced; emergency and pharmacy services typically remain operational as per branch arrangements — please call ahead if unsure.
